SHINE CONSULTANT AND CONSTRUCTION

SHINE CONSULTAND AND CONSTRUCTION PVT LTD is a real estate developer & consultant company, operator and investment manager based in Birgunj, NEPAL. Originally founded in 2021 by Er. PRAMANAND PANDIT, Shine Consultant and Construction Pvt Ltd has evolved into one of the largest and most experienced real estate developers in Province No. 2, Nepal with a proven track record of successful investments. As a fully integrated firm, Shine Consultant and Construction Pvt Ltd has control over every stage of a project’s life cycle including the acquisition, permitting and entitlement process to construction through stabilization. Shine Consultant and Construction Pvt Ltd innovation and creativity in the marketplace has allowed us to develop properties across all asset classes including industrial, manufacturing, biotech, residential, healthcare, education, mixed‐use, and retail.
